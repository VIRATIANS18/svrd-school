About Our Institution

Swami Vivekanand Rashtriya Dnyanpeeth, Jalgaon (Jamod), established in 1998 under the Navyug Education Society, is a reputed educational institution dedicated to academic excellence and value-based education.

The school provides education from Nursery to Secondary level, following a structured, student-centric approach aligned with national educational standards and best practices observed in CBSE-pattern institutions.

Our Vision

To nurture responsible, confident and knowledgeable citizens by providing a balanced education that promotes intellectual growth, moral values, discipline and social responsibility.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer a safe, supportive and stimulating learning environment where students are encouraged to explore their potential, develop critical thinking skills and cultivate lifelong learning habits.

Core Values

Academic Excellence

Encouraging curiosity, innovation and strong conceptual understanding.

Discipline & Ethics

Instilling integrity, respect and responsibility in every student.

Holistic Development

Balancing academics, sports, arts and personality development.

Technology-Enabled Learning

Modern computer labs and digital learning resources.
