Academic Vision & Philosophy

At Swami Vivekanand Rashtriya Dnyanpeeth, Jalgaon (Jamod), academics are rooted in the philosophy of holistic education, where intellectual rigor is balanced with ethical values and emotional well-being.

Our curriculum framework follows CBSE-aligned best practices, emphasizing conceptual understanding, analytical thinking, problem-solving skills and application-based learning.

Teaching Methodology

The teaching-learning process at SVRD integrates traditional classroom instruction with modern pedagogical tools to enhance student engagement and comprehension.

Classroom practices include interactive discussions, project-based learning, laboratory demonstrations, visual aids and continuous formative assessment.

Assessment & Evaluation

Student evaluation follows a continuous and comprehensive approach to assess both scholastic and co-scholastic development.

  • Periodic assessments and unit tests
  • Practical examinations and project work
  • Regular assignments and class participation
  • Structured preparation for board examinations

Academic Enrichment & Support

To support diverse learning needs, the school provides academic enrichment programmes, remedial classes and mentoring support under the guidance of experienced educators.

Regular parent-teacher interactions ensure collaborative monitoring of academic progress and overall student development.
