Admission FAQs

Q: When does admission open?

A: Admissions are open from January through April every year for all classes.

Q: What documents are required?

A: Birth certificate, previous school transfer certificate, Aadhar card, and passport photo.

Q: Is there a registration fee?

A: Yes, registration fee applies which is non-refundable and communicated at the time of enquiry.
